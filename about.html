Car Company

A Quikride Web App that offers users an easier way to rent a car/motorcycle anywhere in the Philippines. Imagine if you’re from Manila and you’re planning to go on a vacation in Bohol and plan to stay there for a week, but you don’t want to avail travel packages offered by travel agencies. Whereas, you just want to explore the whole place on your own. The problem is you don’t have a ride, or you don’t trust the local rent-a-car place that asks too many requirements and questions before they allow you to lend their car, or you probably want to travel the area on a motorcycle, but no one offers a rent-a-motorcycle service.

That is where the Quickride web app helps. This will allow you to make a reservation early, an easy means of payment ,you feel like a local and even choose your own rental vehicle. You'll be able to register as either a renter or a vehicle owner. Also, our Quickride app provides an easy registration process by making sure that the identity check and verification will only be made once through registering an account. This is in contrast with the identity check and verification every time you utilize the service at your local rent-a-car shop.
